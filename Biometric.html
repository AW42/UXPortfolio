OVERVIEW

The AL Habib Biometric App was a project aimed at simplifying the biometric verification process for customers of Bank AL Habib Ltd. The app was built to provide a hassle-free way for customers to complete the verification process from the comfort of their own homes, without needing to visit a bank branch. By utilizing biometric technology, the app allows customers to securely verify their identities using their fingerprints.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • User Research
  • Wireframing
  • Usability Testing

TIMELINE

  • 1.5 Months
  • Dec 2022 - Jan 2023

The Challenge

While working on this project, I discovered that there were no direct competitors (in banking niche) offering this service. However, there were other apps available that offered finger scanning feature, irrespective of their primary purpose or goal. In order to identify pain points and user struggles, it was helpful for me to analyze the insights and user experiences of those apps

So I explored Pak Identity App. It's an app which helps Pakistani citizens in managing their identity documents. To ensure the security of users' personal information they provide biometric verification feature






FINDINGS

I was curious to know about the experiences users had while interacting with this feature and if there was anything that affected their interaction.

Although there were guidelines for scanning the hands but these were the things which frustrated the users



Fingerprint scanning fails even with still fingers and camera.

The app lags while taking the fingerprints.

The app did not recognize their fingerprints or gave an error message.




PERSONAS

The personas were then made after getting to know the experiences users had with this feature in Pak Identity App.

Wireframes & Prototypes

After completing the competitive audit and gathering all necessary information, we began developing the user flow for our app. Our focus was on creating a seamless and intuitive user experience that leverages the latest in biometric technology to provide users with a secure and convenient authentication solution.
