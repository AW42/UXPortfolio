OVERVIEW

The AL Habib Digital Account opening app is an innovative project that aims to transform the traditional account opening process into a hassle-free and seamless experience for customers. The app was designed to address the challenges and pain points that customers face when opening an account in-person. By leveraging the power of mobile technology, the app provides a convenient and accessible solution that allows customers to open their accounts from the comfort of their own homes.

Opening A Bank Account The Old Fashioned Way

Let's face it, in Pakistan, most of the times it won't be a smooth process whenever you have to open your bank account. I really wanted to figure out what it's like getting it done the old fashioned way for which interviews were conducted with those people who matched the following characteristics:

  • Age: 20-45 years old

  • Occupation: Students, working professionals, business owners, and freelancers

  • Income level: Middle to upper-middle class

    ACCOUNT TYPE SELECTION

    When the user chooses the type of account they want to have, whether its individual account or minor account, in both cases users will have to provide their CNIC.

    Since minor can't have one therefore they will need their guardian's CNIC. Now this is something that should be mentioned so that there's no confusion.



    BRANCH SELECTION

    As in the manual branch selection, if the user wants to select any specific branch, they may have to scroll down to find their desired branch, which could be a bit hassle and time consuming if you have a large number of branches in the city.

    Therefore, we replaced the dropdown with a search bar so that the searching is easy and efficient for the user.



    PROGRESS INDICATOR

    Although, the users will able to see the steps that they've completed along with the remaining steps, we decided to add a small percentage indicator.

    This process surely takes time due to its requirements and other formalities and we don't want users to get frustrated. We want them to keep going.
