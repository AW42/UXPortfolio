OVERVIEW

The Al Habib Mobile Banking app serves as a digital platform enabling users to perform various financial transactions and operations conveniently. However, users often encounter frustrations and challenges while using this app. To gain insights into these issues and explore improvement opportunities, I conducted an in-depth analysis to identify the specific areas where users face difficulties and propose potential solutions.

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • User Research
  • Interviews
  • Evaluation
  • Personas

TIMELINE

  • 1 Month
Uncovering Users' Pain Points

Before conducting the interviews, I went through some reviews on both Google play and App Store and was kind of surprised after seeing the overall ratings which is quite alarming as these low ratings indicate a considerable dissatisfaction among users and signal potential issues with the app's performance and user experience.


KEY RESEARCH QUESTIONS

  • How would you rate the overall user experience of the app?

  • How frequently do you use the Al Habib mobile banking app?

  • What are the main features or services you use within the app?

  • What difficulties do you face while using the app?




    • HOW WE INTERVIEWED

    I recruited a total of 16 participants for the interviews by leveraging various methods, including social media messaging, reaching out to relatives, friends, and other individuals.



    FINDINGS FROM THE INTERVIEWS

    These insights closely align with the concerns raised by users on the Play Store and App Store reviews, highlighting the consistent challenges faced by Al Habib mobile banking app users.



    The UI is challenging and painful to use, lacking user-friendliness.

    Frequent app crashes, slow loading times, and server issues, not letting to perform transactions smoothly and efficiently.

    Mostly used for transferring funds and paying bills.



    App never loads smoothly even if we have a good internet connection. Takes forever to just check our balance.

    The overall user experience of the app is rated as poor or frustrating by the users.

    Majority of people would gladly switch to different banks for a better experience.

    User Personas

    After knowing the pain points and having insights from the interviews, we created our 3 personas shown below.

    So What's Next From Here?

    The existing mobile banking application fully relies on the OBDX system, therefore, we have come to realization that a newly developed application will be needed to overcome the limitations of the current system.


    1. EASY TO FOLLOW NAVIGATION

    Implement a proper and easy-to-follow navigation structure, ensuring that users can easily find the desired features and perform tasks without confusion.


    2. SMOOTH TASK EXECUTION

    Design a smooth user flow for various features, ensuring a seamless experience from login to accessing different functionalities, such as money transfers, bill payments, and other banking services.

    3. ESSENTIAL FEATURES ONLY

    Include only the essential features that users require, avoiding unnecessary clutter and complexity. By focusing on core functionalities, the app will load faster and provide a more focused and efficient user experience.
